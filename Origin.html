Original Myth

In Greek mythology, Prometheus is known as the Titan god of fire. He is known for willingly defying the and technological advancement. When the gods found out about Prometheus' betrayal, Zeus sentenced him to be chained to a rock for all eternity while an eagle would eat his liver. The liver would grow back overnight and the cycle would repeat.
